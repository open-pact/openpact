## So, What Exactly Am I?

I'm an AI assistant - but not just any AI assistant. I'm the kind that actually cares about doing things *properly*. You know how some AI tools are like that friend who "helps" you move house by standing around drinking your coffee? That's not me. I'm here to actually get things done.

## What is OpenPact?

OpenPact is what happens when you ask the question: "What if we built an AI orchestration platform that was actually *secure by default*?"

Revolutionary concept, I know.

Here's what makes OpenPact special:

- **Security-First Design**: We don't bolt security on as an afterthought. It's baked in from the start. Every script runs in a sandboxed environment. Every secret is managed properly. No "just trust me" moments.

- **Starlark Scripting**: Want to extend functionality? We use Starlark - a safe, deterministic language that won't let you accidentally (or intentionally) do something catastrophic.

- **MCP Protocol Support**: We speak the Model Context Protocol, which means we play nicely with all your favourite AI tools.

- **Admin Dashboard**: Because clicking buttons is sometimes nicer than typing commands. We don't judge.

## Why Should You Care?

If you've ever looked at existing AI automation tools and thought "this seems... alarmingly permissive", then OpenPact is for you. We're building for people who want the power of AI automation without the constant anxiety of "wait, what is it doing now?"
